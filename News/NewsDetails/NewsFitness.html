Event news Sports and bodybuilding details

Swimming is a sport for everyone of all ages and is not too expensive, helping you improve your health, even your spirit.
1. Sports are both fun and inclusive.

-Exercise and physical activity are fun, with the opportunity to relax, enjoy the outdoors, or simply engage in activities that make you happy. Physical activity can also help you connect with family or friends in a fun social environment.

- Exercise not only helps fight disease, it also creates a stronger heart – the most important muscle in the body. That makes exercise – and activities of daily living – feel easier.

-Your heart and cardiovascular system will work more efficiently. Less plaque will build up in your heart. Your heart will become a more efficient pump. And as your heart gets stronger, it pumps more blood with each beat, so your resting heart rate will be lower.

2. Firm body thanks to gym

-One of the undeniable benefits of gym training is muscle toning. Normally, when exercising on a regular schedule combined with a reasonable diet, it will help you easily build a balanced body.

- Especially for those who are overweight, gym is an extremely effective solution because the exercises will help you burn excess fat and unnecessary calories. Thanks to that, your weight loss process is no longer too difficult, however, perseverance and effort are very important factors.

- For men, going to the gym can stimulate muscle growth, while eliminating weak muscles, creating a muscular body. In addition, going to the gym also helps improve the measurements of women. Many people have succeeded in building their body shape thanks to going to the gym. Therefore, if you are still wondering which sport to choose to exercise your body, the gym is the right choice.

3. Relieve stress - improve brain

- Stress is one of the reasons that reduces the quality of your life. Therefore, preventing or relieving anxiety and sadness in the inner world is very necessary. According to doctors, in addition to entertainment, going to the gym is also a method to help you overcome pressure, tension and balance your mental life.

- In addition, maintaining a gym routine also helps you maintain clarity as well as improve the functions that the brain performs. In fact, when exercising, your heart rate will increase, while promoting the transport of oxygen and blood to the brain. Thanks to that, the brain is stimulated and creates conditions to produce more hormones to promote the development of cells around the brain.

- Some doctors share that when you work out hard, you can reduce the risk of changes in the structure of your brain. Meanwhile, if the brain changes too much, it can increase the risk of some mental illnesses, typically Alzheimer's or schizophrenia. In addition, the gym is also a factor that helps the process of transporting blood to the brain more smoothly to improve memory and maintain your health.
