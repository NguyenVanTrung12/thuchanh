Event news Group Fitness

Participating in group exercise not only reduces stress levels but also achieves many other notable physical health benefits . In addition, social relationships also become stronger.
1. Is group exercise better than exercising alone?.

-Overall, participating in group exercise not only reduces the risk ofstressbut also achieve many other remarkable physical health benefits. In addition, social relationships also become more connected. Communicating and motivating each other to exercise helps you increase your willpower and want to exercise more. Because exercising in a large group is considered a fun social activity, helps satisfy basic psychological needs and supports strengthening each person's responsibility to exercise voluntarily.

-Another scientific report also shows that individuals who exercise in groups often have a better pain tolerance than those who exercise alone. This is due to the body releasing more hormones.Endorphinwhen all team members get along with each other during practice..

2. How to Get the Most Out of Group Exercise

-Health Check: Before joining any class or exercise group, check your health status and discuss with your doctor if necessary.

-Choose the right workout location: Choose a workout location that fits your goals and personal preferences. This could be a gym, a park, or even a specific sports club..

- Pre-Workout Snack: Make sure you eat a light meal before your workout to keep yourself energized. Avoid eating too much before your workout to avoid feeling full.

-Stretching: Take time to stretch before and after your workout. This helps reduce your risk of injury and increases your flexibility.

-Vary your workouts: To avoid getting your body used to a certain workout routine, change up your workouts and training regimen periodically. This helps ensure that every muscle in your body is developed evenly.

Exercising in a group has many benefits for your mental and physical health. However, to get the most out of it, you need to choose an exercise style that suits your goals and follow basic principles such as getting a health check, choosing the right place to exercise, eating a snack before your workout, stretching, and changing your workouts regularly. Joining an exercise group and understanding how to exercise properly will also help you achieve more physical and mental health benefits.

3. Sports and exercise prevent disease and disability

- No matter what your current weight is, being physically active boosts your levels of High-Density Lipoprotein (HDL) – the good blood fat – and reduces triglycerides, the unhealthy Low-Density Lipoprotein (LDLc), a dual effect that helps keep your blood flowing and reduces your risk of heart disease..

- Regular exercise helps prevent or control many health problems, including:

+ Stroke

+ Metabolic syndrome

+ High blood pressure

+ Systemic atherosclerosis

+ Type 2 diabetes

+ Anxiety disorders ...

4. Exercise increases energy

- Regular physical activity can improve your muscle strength and increase your endurance.

-Exercise increases the delivery of oxygen and nutrients to your tissues and helps your cardiovascular system work more efficiently. And when your heart and lung health improve, you have more energy to tackle everyday tasks.
