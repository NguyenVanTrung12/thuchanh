Swimming is a sport for everyone of all ages and is not too expensive, helping you improve your health, even your spirit.
1. Is swimming good?

- Swimming is considered one of the best forms of physical exercise. When practiced, it can affect most muscles without having to endure any harmful effects on the joints. In addition, swimming is often added to the training program by athletes to maintain endurance and keep fit while recovering from injuries.

- If you practice swimming regularly, you will gradually gain muscle strength throughout your entire body. When swimming underwater, you need to coordinate the use of many muscle groups together to be able to move your body, thereby making the whole body move, improving strength and endurance.
